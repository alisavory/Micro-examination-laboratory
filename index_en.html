About Us

Accurate Testing Lab is a structural engineering lab offering testing services for materials, concrete, soil, and steel, following Iraqi and international standards. Our mission is to ensure high-quality materials for engineering and construction projects in Wasit and surrounding areas.

Our Services

  • Concrete tests (compression, tension, flexural)
  • Soil tests (analysis and improvement)
  • Steel tests (tensile, bending, reinforcement)
  • Technical and engineering reports
  • Site visits and sampling

Forms & Reports

We offer official templates for documenting test results. Contact us or visit our lab to request a specific report.
